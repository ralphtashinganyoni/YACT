Yamurai AIDS Care Trust Timeline

  • The Journey Begins

    March 2018

    Yamurai Aids Care Trust (YACT) is a community based trust that was founded in early 2018 by individuals amongst which where pcs working in Dzivarasekwa. The PC’s during their work in Dzivarasekwa Extension noted with deep concern the plight of those infected and affected by HIV/AIDs in particular orphans.

  • Burning Desire

    In coming together, the group further noted that the situation in Dzivarasekwa was however not peculiar to DZ alone but rather a phenomenon in Zimbabwe.The desire to assist those in need led to the formation of Yamurai Aids Care Trust. The desire to assist those in need led to the formation of Yamurai Aids Care Trust.

  • AIM

    YACT’s main aim is to provide care and psycho-social support to those living with and those affected by HIV and AIDS especially orphans complementing the efforts of the community and other organizations in the field.

  • Vision

    YACT strives to provide care to orphans and care givers through primary counseling and psycho-social support through post counseling care.

  • Mission

    YACT will provide care to orphans and children living with HIV through post counseling care including provisions of basic food stuffs, assistance in education, clothing and a decent place to live.
