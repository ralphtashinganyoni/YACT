Operational Strategy

YACT operates mainly in Dzivarasekwa which is in Zvimba Rural District. YACT works in child friendly counseling centres and will provide counseling within the community,but will not limit the services to Zvimba alone. YACT will also work in conjunction with other organisations within and outside Dzivarasekwa to provide care and support to the orphans affected or infected by HIV/AIDS.

STIGMATIZATION

Stigma associated with HIV and AIDS related orphaned children.“Some people when they hear that someone’s HIV positive – especially us Africans – they’ll be seeing someone who’s dying, someone who is not supposed to touch anyone.” Stigma leads to people not being treated with dignity and respect.

CHILD HEADED FAMILIES

The HIV/AIDS epidemic has led to many situations where both parents have died and left behind a family of orphans.The children may begin taking over their parent's responsibilities before the last surviving parent has died, when the parent is sick or too weak to work. The eldest children often have to stop going to school and get a job instead in order to gain income for the family

WHY DZIVARASEKWA ?

Dzivarasekwa Extension is a high density suburb located in the West of Harare. In the high density area there is a high prevalence of HIV and AIDS.. This has culminated into a high HIV related deaths leading to an increase in the number of HIV related orphans. While this has continued in Zimbabwe and the high density have not been spared

LACK OF COUNSELLING

Counselling before and after being tested for HIV is a great opportunity to share important health information that can save lives. By using this guide community health care professionals may improve client service skills by: Understanding informed consent and its implications.

CHILD PROSTITUTION

Child prostitution is one of the most sinister and horrific acts that children may endure. It presents serious violations of human and child rights on children who are among the most vulnerable and impressionable in society.

Aim

To complement the efforts of the community and other organisations towards HIV and AIDS victim orphans.

Vision

To provide care to orphans and care givers and psycho-social support through primary and post counselling

Mission

YACT will support through post counseling care including provisions of basic food stuffs, assistance in education, clothing and a decent place to live.

Key challenges relative to HIV and AIDS in Dzivarasekwa

  • Transactional sex to earn a living for HIV orphans.

  • Child headed families.

  • Stigma associated with HIV and AIDS related orphaned children.

  • Increased poverty as a result of lack of support for HIV and AIDS orphaned children
